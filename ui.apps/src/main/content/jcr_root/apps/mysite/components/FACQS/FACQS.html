SkyGo – Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
What is SkyGo?
SkyGo is a seamless flight booking platform that helps travelers find, compare, and book domestic and international flights at the best prices.
How do I book a flight on SkyGo?
Simply enter your departure and destination cities, select your travel dates, and choose from a list of available flights. Follow the prompts to complete your booking.
Can I cancel or modify my booking?
Yes, you can manage your booking through the “My Trips” section. Cancellation and modification policies depend on the airline and fare type.
Does SkyGo offer refunds?
Refund eligibility depends on the airline’s policy. Refundable tickets will be processed within 7–10 business days.
Is it safe to book through SkyGo?
Absolutely. SkyGo uses secure payment gateways and complies with global data protection standards to ensure your information is safe.
Can I book multi-city or round-trip flights?
Yes, SkyGo supports one-way, round-trip, and multi-city bookings.
Do I need to create an account to book a flight?
No, but creating an account allows you to track bookings, receive alerts, and access exclusive deals.
How do I contact SkyGo customer support?
You can reach us via live chat, email, or our 24/7 helpline listed on the Contact Us page.
