PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

Sky Go values your concern for privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of our customers. We want to assure you that we follow appropriate standards when it comes to safeguarding your privacy on our sales channels.

Cookies

Our web pages use "cookies" to serve you better by customizing the information based on your preferences. Cookies help identify returning users and streamline your next visit to our site. The information collected through cookies is used solely to analyze and improve our services for you.

Type of Information We Collect

We collect the following personal and sensitive information when you transact with us to provide the services you choose:

  • Age

Sky Go does not share or disclose any of the above sensitive information without the explicit permission of its users or customers.

As passengers, you are ultimately utilizing the services provided by the airline. Sky Go will not be held liable for any disruptions or changes to your travel plans initiated by the airline or other service providers.

How We Use Personal Information

The above-mentioned information is collected from users/customers/travelers for the following purposes:

While Making a Booking

We may use personal information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, and age details. These are shared with related service providers including airlines, hotels, or bus services to facilitate reservations and bookings for customers or travelers.

Marketing Promotions

Sky Go uses details like mobile numbers and email IDs to send information about promotional offers. We may sponsor promotions and lucky draws to give members an opportunity to win discounts on travel or other prizes. Participation is optional, and users can unsubscribe from such emails. In such cases, customers may not receive updates about ongoing promotional discounts.

Opt-out of Promotional Emails

Users can opt out of receiving promotional emails from Sky Go by clicking the unsubscribe link provided in the emails or by updating their communication preferences in their account settings.

Withdrawal of Consent

If you wish to withdraw your consent for the use of your personal information, you may do so by contacting our support team. Please note that withdrawing consent may affect our ability to provide certain services to you.

Rights Regarding Personal Information

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information held by Sky Go. To exercise these rights, please contact our data protection officer or customer support.

Eligibility to Transact

Only individuals who are 18 years of age or older are eligible to make transactions on the Sky Go platform. By using our services, you confirm that you meet this eligibility requirement.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

Sky Go reserves the right to update or modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage users to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting their information.
